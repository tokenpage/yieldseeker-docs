This Privacy Policy describes how **Yield Seeker** ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you access or use our platform ("Interface").

We respect your privacy and are committed to safeguarding your data in a manner consistent with Web3 values: **minimal data collection**, **user control**, and **transparency**.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII) such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers unless you voluntarily provide them (e.g., via customer support).

We may automatically collect:

- Wallet address used to connect to the Interface  
- Device data (e.g., browser type, OS, screen size)  
- Usage data (e.g., pages visited, interactions with the AI agent or platform features)  
- Transaction metadata routed through our platform  

> We do **not** link this data to your real-world identity.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the data we collect to:

- Operate and maintain the Interface  
- Improve platform performance, UX, and stability  
- Detect suspicious activity and enhance security  
- Analyze usage trends to optimize DeFi strategies  
- Respond to technical support requests  

> We do **not** sell your data or share it for advertising purposes.

---

## 3. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We may use limited tracking technologies (such as cookies or local storage) to:

- Maintain session state  
- Track anonymized performance metrics  
- Monitor system health  

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling them may impact functionality.

---

## 4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not share your personal data with third parties, except:

- As required by law, regulation, subpoena, or legal process  
- To cooperate with law enforcement, regulators, or investigations  
- With infrastructure service providers (e.g., cloud or analytics providers), under strict data protection terms  

> We do **not knowingly share user data with jurisdictions subject to international sanctions.**

---

## 5. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary for:

- Platform operation  
- Security auditing  
- Legal compliance  

We strive to minimize storage of any unnecessary or sensitive information.
