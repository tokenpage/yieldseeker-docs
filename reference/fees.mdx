# Fees

Yieldseeker does not currently charge any fees for using the protocol.

There are no subscription fees, deposit fees, withdrawal fees, or performance fees.

A transparent performance-based fee model is planned as a future protocol feature. Any future changes will be communicated in advance.

---

## Performance Fees

Yieldseeker intends to introduce a performance-based fee model as a future protocol feature.

Performance fees will apply only to realised yield generated by the protocol.

If your portfolio does not generate profit, no performance fee will be charged.

Additional details, including fee rates and implementation, will be published before the feature becomes available.

---

## Reward Tokens

Many DeFi protocols distribute rewards using tokens other than the asset originally deposited.

Yieldseeker automatically claims these rewards, converts them where appropriate, and compounds them back into your portfolio.

As a result, users receive withdrawals in the same asset they originally deposited, eliminating the need to manually manage multiple reward tokens.

---

## Transparent Accounting

Fee calculations will be performed on-chain using a cost-basis model.

Users will be able to independently verify fee calculations directly through the protocol's smart contracts.

This approach ensures that fees remain transparent, auditable, and aligned with the protocol's **Verify, Don't Trust** philosophy.
