# Fees

Yield Seeker is designed so that users only pay when value has been created.

There are currently no subscription costs, deposit fees, or withdrawal fees.

---

## Beta Period

Yield Seeker is currently in beta.

During the beta period, the protocol does **not** charge any performance fees.

This allows users to evaluate the platform while helping us continue improving the protocol.

Future fee changes will always be communicated in advance.

---

## Performance Fees

Following the beta period, Yield Seeker intends to introduce a performance-based fee model.

Performance fees will only apply to realised yield generated by the protocol.

If your portfolio does not generate profit, no performance fee will be charged.

---

## Reward Tokens

Many DeFi protocols distribute rewards using tokens other than the asset originally deposited.

Yield Seeker automatically claims these rewards, converts them where appropriate, and compounds them back into your portfolio.

As a result, users always receive withdrawals in the same asset they originally deposited, eliminating the need to manually manage multiple reward tokens.

---

## Transparent Accounting

Fee calculations are performed on-chain using a cost-basis model.

Users can independently verify outstanding fees directly through the protocol's smart contracts.

This ensures that fee calculations remain transparent and auditable.
